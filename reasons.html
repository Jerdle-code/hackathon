Environmental Problems

Climate Change

Causes:

The main driver of climate change is the emission of greenhouse gases (GHGs). One of the main GHGs in the Earth's atmosphere is carbon dioxide (CO2). That is why it is so important to reduce CO2 emissions.

Effects:

Temperatures in the Arctic are rising. As a result, polar ice caps and glaciers are melting, making it extremely difficult for polar bears to find food.

Have you noticed that extreme weather events occur more frequently nowadays? That is also related to climate change. In 2020, 13 hurricanes have hit the North Atlantic, 6 of them major. Those have led to a total death toll of over 431 people and a total damage of around $51 billion.

Pollution

Causes:

Pollution can be natural, such as volcanic ash. But it can also be caused by human activities, such as car exhaust and industrial discharge.

Effects:

Pollution causes around 7 million premature deaths every year resulting from long-term health problems such as lung cancer, respiratory diseases, heart diseases, etc. According to WHO, "9 out of 10 people breathe air that exceeds WHO guideline limits containing high levels of pollutants, with low- and middle-income countries suffering from the highest exposures."

Waste

Plastic:

Plastic products such as plastic bags and cups release toxic chemicals into the soil and damage the environment. Sadly, 100,000 marine animals are killed by waste plastic products every year. One in three sea turtles have been found with plastic in their stomachs.

Water

Water waste is another major issue. Since water takes a long process to be filtered and cleaned to become drinkable, we now have much less usable water left on the planet. According to WHO, more than 3.4 million people die each year from waterborne diseases, and this kind of disease is caused by filthy water.

Paper

93% of paper comes from trees, and trees release oxygen through the process of photosynthesis. Approximately 1 billion trees worth of paper (1.85 million tons) are thrown away every year in the U.S alone.
